How It Works

1

NLP Preprocessing

Tokenisation, stopword removal, and lemmatisation normalise the text before analysis.

2

TF-IDF Vectorisation

Each article is converted to a high-dimensional numerical feature vector using bigram TF-IDF.

3

ML Classification

Logistic Regression and Passive Aggressive classifiers vote on whether the article is fake or real.

4

Confidence Score

A probability (or sigmoid-normalised score) represents how certain the model is in its verdict.
